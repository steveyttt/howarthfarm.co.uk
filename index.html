CAMPING ON THE FARM

"A proper working farm in North Yorkshire"

Welcome to Howarth Farm, a functioning farm and campsite set in the heart of the Yorkshire Dales. We have plots for tents, campervans and caravans and run from March until the cold gets too much! (Usually October). Onsite we have toilets and showers but no electrical hookups for tents or campervans. Static caravans can be rented on a long term basis, please send any queries about caravans to the e-mail address in the contact us section. See below for prices and also a list of local activities, North yorkshire has lots to offer! We do not allow large groups of adults or fires. Please note this is a functioning farm with machinery, tractors, cows, dogs & chickens on site. The local towns of Skipton & Grassington are close by and great for day visits.
